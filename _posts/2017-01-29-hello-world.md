_There were several roads nearby, but it did not take her long to find the one paved with yellow bricks. Within a short time she was walking briskly toward the Emerald City, her silver shoes tinkling merrily on the hard, yellow road-bed._ -- The Wonderful Wizard of Oz

Road of yellow bricks has led Dorothy to the Emerald city, where she sought a help from The Wizard of Oz for returning her back to Kansas. Down to the road, Dorothy met _Scarecrow_, who wants a _brain_; _Tin Woodman_, who wants a _heart_; and _Cowardly Lion_, who wants _courage_.
